Why Choose Window Blinds

We offer custom quality louvres made from a wide selection of fabrics. Different control systems are also available, making the blinds easier and safer to use.

Each louvre on our vertical blinds can be rotated 180 degrees, providing the desired level of shade and privacy through angling.


As a homeowner, you can really reap the benefits of vertical blinds due to their economical value. Vertical blinds will cover larger expanses of windows usually at an affordable, cost-effective rate for the homeowner.

Vertical blinds allow you the option of controlling your home’s privacy whilst still allowing for natural light and warmth from the sun


Here at Skookum Investments Limited, we manufacture our own vertical blinds in a workshop purpose built for creating a high-quality product.
